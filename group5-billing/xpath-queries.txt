====================================================================
SARMS - Smart Academic Records Management System
Group 5: Student Billing System
XPath Queries (Minimum 5 Required)
====================================================================

The following XPath expressions are designed to extract and filter
data from billing.xml. Each query corresponds to the specific items
found in the provided XPath-Query document.  

Context document: billing.xml

--------------------------------------------------------------------
QUERY 1: Retrieve students with unpaid balances (Required by Case Study)
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Expression:
/billing/record [balance > 0]

Explanation:
Filters records to identify students who have any remaining
debt higher than zero.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
QUERY 2: Retrieve fully paid accounts
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Expression:
/billing/record [balance = 0]

Explanation:
Filters the records to identify students who have completely 
paid off their tuition with zero remaining balance.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
QUERY 3: Retrieve all student IDs
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Expression:
/billing/record/studentId   

Explanation:
Selects all unique student identification numbers from the
billing records.  

--------------------------------------------------------------------
QUERY 4: Retrieve all tuition fees
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Expression:
/billing/record/tuitionFee   

Explanation:
Retrieves the total tuition fee amount for every student
entry.  

--------------------------------------------------------------------
QUERY 5: Retrieve all balances
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Expression:
/billing/record/balance   

Explanation:
Extracts the current balance values for all student accounts.  

--------------------------------------------------------------------
QUERY 6: Retrieve all billing records
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Expression:
/billing/record   

Explanation:
Selects every billing record entry available in the system.
  
--------------------------------------------------------------------
QUERY 7: Retrieve students with balance greater than 20,000
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Expression:
/billing/record [balance > 20000]   

Explanation:
Identifies students with high outstanding balances exceeding
20,000.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 8: Retrieve students with balance less than 5,000
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Expression:
/billing/record [balance < 5000]   

Explanation:
Selects students who have almost cleared their accounts with a
remaining balance under 5,000.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 9: Retrieve students who paid more than 30,000
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
/billing/record [paymentsMade > 30000]   
 
Explanation:
Filters for students whose total payments made exceed
30,000.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 10: Retrieve students with tuition fee above 45,000
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
/billing/record [tuition Fee > 45000]   

Explanation:
Selects records where the total tuition assessment is higher
than 45,000.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 11: Retrieve students whose payment is greater than their balance
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
/billing/record [paymentsMade > balance]   

Explanation:
Identifies students who have paid more than their current
outstanding balance.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 12: Retrieve students whose balance is greater than payments made
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
/billing/record [balance > paymentsMade]   

Explanation:
Identifies students whose remaining debt is currently higher
than the amount they have paid.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 13: Retrieve students whose balance is more than half of the tuition fee
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
/billing/record [balance > tuitionFee div 2]   

Explanation:
Uses mathematical division to find students who still owe
more than 50% of their total tuition.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 14: Retrieve records where tuition fee equals payments made plus balance
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
/billing/record [tuitionFee = paymentsMade + balance]   

Explanation:
Used for data validation to ensure the total fee matches the
sum of the payment and the debt.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 15: Retrieve the first billing record
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
/billing/record [1]   

Explanation:
Selects only the very first record entry in the XML
structure.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 16: Retrieve the last billing record
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
/billing/record [last()]   

Explanation:
Uses the last() function to retrieve the final entry in the
billing file.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 17: Count the total number of billing records
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
count(/billing/record)   

Explanation:
Calculates the total quantity of records currently in the
system.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 18: Count students with unpaid balances above 20,000
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
count(/billing/record [balance > 20000])   

Explanation:
Provides a numerical count of students holding a high
debt status.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 19: Retrieve student record by specific ID
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
/billing/record [contains(studentId, '00045')]   

Explanation:
A string search that returns the record for the specific student 
ID containing "00045".

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 20: Retrieve students from batch 2023
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 

Expression:
/billing/record [starts-with(studentId, '23-')]   

Explanation:
Filters records where the studentId begins exactly with "23-",
representing the enrollment batch year.

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 21: Retrieve students whose IDs end with '00'
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
Expression:
/billing/record [substring(studentId, string-length(studentId)-1, 2) = '00']   

Explanation:
A string manipulation that isolates the end of the studentId
to match specific sequences.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 22: Retrieve duplicate student records (Integrity Check)
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
Expression:
/billing/record [studentId = preceding::record/studentId]   

Explanation:
Checks previous records to find duplicate entries with identical
student IDs.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 23: Retrieve students with exact balance of 15,875
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
Expression:
/billing/record [balance = 15875]   

Explanation:
Performs an exact numerical match for a specific balance
amount.  

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 24: Retrieve records with unusually low tuition figures
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
Expression:
/billing/record [string-length(tuitionFee) < 5]   

Explanation:
Filters students based on the total character count of
their tuition fee (e.g. less than 5 digits means under 10,000).

-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
QUERY 25: Retrieve student IDs and balances together
-------------------------------------------------------------------- 
Expression:
/billing/record/studentId | /billing/record/balance   

Explanation:
Uses the union operator to combine two node sets for a summary
view.  

====================================================================
END OF XPATH QUERIES
